Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI)

Objectives

Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI) was established to serve as a communication platform for the management teams, shareholders, securities analysts, capital markets, and governing agencies that work with listed companies at stock exchanges, over-the-counter markets, and emerging markets, as well as companies planning public offerings and non-public innovative companies. We seek to advance investor relations in Taiwan and the professional capacity and positioning of our members, and international capital market communication.

Milestones

  • 2025 Leading a Smart New Era of IR
    TIRI marked a new era of intelligent IR with the first TIRI ceremony and the 2025 TIRI Awards, bringing together global insights from Extel, S&P Global, and Bloomberg to shape the future of data-driven investor communication.
  • 2024 Global Vision Co-creating a New Era of IR
    TIRI launched a new chapter for IR with the International IR Connectivity Ceremony and the 2024 TIRI Awards, further strengthening cross-border collaboration through an international summit uniting IR leaders from Europe and the Asia-Pacific region.
  • 2023 Global IR Experts Confluence in Taiwan
    Annual TIRI Summit and International Forum for Leading Insights with 6 region IR associations leader.​
  • 2022 Organizing the 1st Taiwan Investor Relations Excellence Awards
    Selected by hundreds of institutional investors and media, including top IR companies and individuals like TSMC, to receive recognition and attend the ceremony.
  • 2021 Strengthening the integration of international IR practices
    With the vigorous promotion of TIRI, 4 winners have been born in IRC Taiwan.
  • 2020 Innovative service
    In response to the outbreak of Covid, TIRI are first institute issued online courses for BODs, corporate governance supervisors, and IRO.
  • 2019 Expand domestic and foreign influence
    Hong Kong, Singapore IR Association Chairman attended annual Conference. Held our inaugural meeting, first general assembly, and elections for the Board of Directors and Board of Supervisors
  • 2018 Established
    Asia Director of New York Stock Exchange (NYSE), CEO of National Investor Relations Institute (NIRI), GM of Taiwan Stock Exchange, and hundreds VIPs from capital market and industry join annual conference.
