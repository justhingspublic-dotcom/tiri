2025 Annual Conference: Leading a Smart New Era of IR

2025 Annual Conference: Leading a Smart New Era of IR

Professional lecturers attending the conference
Date & Time
Thursday, October 23, 2025 | 2:00 PM – 8:00 PM
Venue
Grand Ballroom (3rd Floor), Shangri-La, Taipei
(No. 201, Section 2, Dunhua South Road, Da’an District, Taipei)
Background & Theme
Looking ahead to 2025, TIRI presents the annual conference under the theme “Smart-Driven Future: Co-Creating a New Era of IR.” The theme reflects the profound impact of digital transformation and artificial intelligence on capital markets. The Institute aims to collaborate with domestic and international partners to harness innovative technologies, deepen investor engagement, enhance corporate value communication, and usher in a new age for IR characterized by intelligence and globalization.
