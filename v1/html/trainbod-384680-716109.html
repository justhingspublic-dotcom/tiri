【Course Sharing】Continuing Education Course for Accounting Executives

​​[Course Sharing] Continuing Education Classes for Accounting Supervisors/Publishers/Brokerages/Stock Exchanges

Accounting supervisors are required to complete a 12-hour continuing education course annually. Traditionally, this involves extensive travel for in-person classes. With the ongoing impact of the pandemic, it has become particularly challenging for supervisors abroad to return to Taiwan.

To make continuing education more accessible and effective, Shih Chien University will launch a series of physical and online courses in 2024. Stay tuned for more details!

We invite accounting supervisors from all financial institutions who are required to pursue annual continuing education to register. Participants who meet the evaluation criteria will receive a certification of completion.

Shih Chien University is recognized by the Financial Supervisory Commission, Executive Yuan, as an accredited institution for professional training courses for accounting supervisors in issuers, brokerages, and stock exchanges. It was approved in 2020 under the official document number 1090346226.
This course is hosted by Shih Chien University. This information is shared for course promotion and dissemination purposes only.
