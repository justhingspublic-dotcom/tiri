2024Global Vision Co-Creating a New Era for IR

2024.12.05 Global Vision Co-Creating a New Era for IR

 2024.12.06 Visiting TWSE,Taiwan Stock Museum,and M-Power Information Co., Ltd.

Greetings to the attending guests
Date
Thursday, December 5th, 2024, 14:00–20:00
Location
Regent Taipei Hotel, 3rd Floor Banquet Hall (3F, No. 3, Lane 39, Section 2, Zhongshan North Road, Zhongshan District, Taipei City)
Background
The 2024 event highlights the power of IR (Investor Relations) in connecting Asia with the world, emphasizing collaboration and integration in the global capital markets through the field of Investor Relations. This year, we have invited several international IR associations to Taiwan. During the annual conference on December 5th, we will foster global perspectives through mutual learning and collaboration, promoting international cooperation and joint development in IR. We celebrate diversity from different cultures and investment viewpoints and look forward to opening a new era of global collaboration in Investor Relations.
 
The day after the annual conference, we will host visits for our international guests to the Taiwan Stock Exchange, the Taiwan Depository and Clearing Corporation, and the Stock Museum. In addition to professional knowledge exchange, we aim to enhance Taiwan’s soft power through cultural sharing. By visiting the Taiwan Stock Exchange, international guests will gain insights into the measures and advantages of Taiwan’s capital market, further promoting the competitiveness and international exposure of Taiwan’s capital market.
