TIRI Executive-Level IR Practical Elite Program
TIRI Executive-Level IR Practical Elite Program
Designed by the Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI) specifically for corporate senior and mid-level executives, this flagship program is dedicated to enhancing comprehensive professional capabilities in Investor Relations (IR).

Global Alignment • Authoritative Teaching Material

Global Alignment • Authoritative Teaching Material
The program adopts the Traditional Chinese edition of the classic textbook Investor Relations Body of Knowledge, 2nd Edition, officially authorized by the National Investor Relations Institute (NIRI, USA).
This edition is the first-ever NIRI-authorized Chinese-language version worldwide, marking a breakthrough milestone in TIRI’s efforts to advance the institutionalization of IR and align professional standards with global practices.
The authorization ceremony was formally launched on June 3, 2025, at the NIRI Annual Conference in Boston, where TIRI Executive Director Jack Chang presented the textbook to NIRI, receiving high international recognition.
👉 Read full media coverage here

Core Strengths of the Program

  • Interdisciplinary Integration
​Focused on practical themes such as financial disclosure, legal and regulatory compliance, media communications, and ESG strategy, institutionalizing the cultivation of IR professionals.
  • Practice-Oriented Training
​Combining case studies and problem diagnosis to equip participants with the ability to address real-world challenges.
  • ​Exclusive Completion Certificate
​​Upon successful completion, participants receive a TIRIC-branded certificate, signifying professional recognition and learning achievement.
Program Status & Future Outlook
  • Ongoing Program
The current cohort of the TIRIC Executive-Level IR Practical Course commenced on August 22, 2025, spanning five weeks. Participants are engaging in in-depth collaboration with leading industry instructors to advance Taiwan’s IR professional practices.
  • Looking Ahead     
TIRI will continue to expand the TIRIC advanced course series, with future schedules and enrollment details announced through the official website.
