2023.11.16 Annual Conference and International Forum

2023.11.17  TIRI Visit to TWSE, TPEx, and National Palace Museum

Greetings to the attending guests
Date
November 16, 2023 (Thursday), 14:30 - 20:00
Location
Regent Taipei Hotel, 3rd Floor Banquet Hall (3F, No. 3, Lane 39, Section 2, Zhongshan North Road, Zhongshan District, Taipei City)
Background
2023 marks the 5th anniversary of the Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI). From November 15 to November 18, TIRI has invited six overseas IR associations to Taiwan. On November 16, an annual conference will be held featuring an international forum to share expertise. This event will also include the launch ceremony for the International Investor Relations Link, the IR Awards, and other activities. These efforts aim to announce Taiwan's role in the global capital market, enhance Taiwan’s international visibility through private sector efforts, and elevate the professionalism of Taiwan’s IR sector.
 
Following the annual conference, on the next day, we invite international guests to visit the Taiwan Stock Exchange, the Over-The-Counter Trading Center, and the National Palace Museum. Besides professional knowledge exchange, cultural sharing sessions are organized to strengthen Taiwan's soft power. By visiting the Taiwan Stock Exchange, international guests will gain insights into the measures and advantages of Taiwan's capital market, further enhancing the competitiveness and international visibility of Taiwan's capital market.
