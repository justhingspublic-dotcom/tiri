Sponsor for IRC©

To encourage and promote expertise in investor relations, TIRI is offering two scholarships of NT$30,000 each in 2024 for individuals who obtain the NIRI IRC certification. Whether you are seeking professional advancement or aspiring to enter the field of investor relations, this is an opportunity not to be missed!

Application Process

  1. Fill out the TIRI application form to qualify for the scholarship application.
          https://forms.gle/FrSrmAM43Td5hf2J6
  1. Register for the IRC exam on the NIRI website.
    https://portal.niri.org/eweb/DynamicPage.aspx?webcode=AGSCertApps
  2. After obtaining the IRC certification, email your IRC e-certificate and payment account details to [email protected].
  3. Once TIRI confirms your IRC certification, we will contact you and deposit the NT$30,000 scholarship into your designated account. Additionally, you will be invited to the annual conference in 2024 to receive recognition.

Additional Information

  • There are two scholarships available, awarded on a first-come, first-served basis to those who provide their IRC e-certificates, until filled.
  • If IRC certificates are provided simultaneously, the application time will serve as the basis for awarding the scholarships.
  • This scholarship is only available to residents of Taiwan.
  • The names of the scholarship recipients will be announced on this webpage to ensure fairness.

Why Choose NIRI's IRC?

NIRI is the world's largest professional investor relations association, representing over 1,500 public companies with a combined market value exceeding $12 trillion. The IRC certification is developed collaboratively by professionals and stakeholders to ensure that certificate holders possess the necessary knowledge and skills within the corporate, institutional, and investment communities.

Professionals in investor relations have significant responsibilities and can make a substantial impact within an organization. This requires expertise in key areas such as finance, strategy, and communication. Obtaining an IRC certification demonstrates that you have the essential knowledge and skills within the investor relations competency framework.

Starting in June 2024, the investor relations competency framework will include eleven critical areas vital to performing investor relations functions, such as strategy development, capital markets, and capital structure. Earning an IRC certificate not only enhances your career but also serves as a professional credential in the field of investor relations. Discover more about the value of IRC certification and seize this rare opportunity!
