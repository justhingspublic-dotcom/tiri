2020 Annual Conference and Launch Ceremony of TIRI IR Awards

2020 Annual Conference and Launch Ceremony of TIRI IR Award

RECAP

Topic

In 2020, the Financial Supervisory Commission announced the Corporate Governance 3.0 - Sustainable Development Blueprint, with the central focus on five major axes: "Enhancing Information Transparency and Promoting Sustainable Operations" and "Strengthening Stakeholder Communication to Foster Good Interaction Channels," among others, to continuously deepen corporate governance in Taiwan and enhance the international competitiveness of our capital markets.

The Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI) actively supports these policy goals by organizing seminars and events to promote the importance of the five main axes of the Sustainable Development Blueprint among listed companies in Taiwan. At this year's annual meeting, we are pleased to announce the launch of the inaugural Taiwan Region TIRI IR Awards in 2021, aimed at recognizing outstanding companies and professionals to elevate standards of excellence and market recognition.

Points of the annual conference：
  • TIRI IR Awards Launch Ceremony"
  • IRC Certification Presentation and Sharing"
  • Investor Relations Survey Program for Taiwan Listed Companies"
  • IR Special Seminar"

Date

December 23, 2020 (Wednesday) 17:30~20:30

Location

Grand Ballroom, 3rd Floor, Taipei Regent Hotel
(No. 3, Lane 39, Section 2, Zhongshan North Road, Zhongshan District, Taipei City)

Agenda

17:45~17:55  Tribute video by foreign guests
17:55~18:00  TIRI IR Awards Launch Ceremony
