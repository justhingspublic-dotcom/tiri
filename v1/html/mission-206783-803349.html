4th TIRI Awards Officially Launched — Calling on Investment Professionals and Financial Media to Participate

To elevate the quality of information disclosure and enhance investor relations (IR) practices in Taiwan’s capital market, the Taiwan Investor Relations Institute (TIRI) is proud to announce the official launch of the 4th TIRI Awards (2025).
Voting is now open and will continue through August 20, 2025. We warmly encourage institutional investors and financial media professionals to participate and cast your votes for Taiwan’s most outstanding IR performers.
Voting Period: Now through August 20, 2025
Eligible Participants:

  • Fund managers and analysts covering Taiwan-listed equities
  • ​Financial journalists reporting on Taiwan’s industries and companies
Each eligible individual may vote independently. There is no limit on the number of participants per organization.
Voting Link and Details
TIRI warmly invites all professionals in the investment and media community to join this important initiative, working together to advance transparency and sustainable development in Taiwan’s capital market.
