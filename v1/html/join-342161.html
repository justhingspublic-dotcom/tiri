Join now and enjoy discounts on courses organized by TIRI
*  Group members will receive two in-house training sessions for directors or 60 hours of free enrollment in public classes, followed by a 50% discount once these are fully booked.
*  Individual members will receive 6 hours of free enrollment, followed by a 10% discount after full capacity.
More exclusive member benefits

Apply for Individual Prospective Member

    *First-time join for individual membership fee is NT$ 8,000
    (Admission fee NT$ 2,000, Annual membership fee NT$ 6,000).
    *Annual membership fee NT$ 6,000 from the next year.
    *Individual permanent membership: Lump-sum payment of NT$60,000 TWD for ten years of annual membership.
SUBMIT

Apply for Individual Formal member

To apply for individual formal membership, please download the application form for individual formal membership from our website, and mail it to TIRI after fill in.
Rights of formal and prospective members are identical except that formal members must attend the general meeting and participate in the election of board and supervisory members.
If you are too busy to attend the general meeting or participate in voting, we recommend applying for prospective membership.
Your application for formal membership will be reviewed by our secretariat and submitted to the board and supervisory meeting for approval. Until approved, you will hold the status of a prospective member.
